Services

With decades of hospitality at our decadent Ambassadors Royal Hotel we aim to inspire as well as accommodate. From years long traditions of PB&J served nightly at 10pm in the lobby to complimentary WIFI and overnight shoeshine, we invite you to claim ourhotel as your southern home in artistic and lavish surroundings. Enjoy views from the roof top pool, dine at our award winning restaurants and work out in the executive fitness center. Our world-class concierge is on hand to help plan outings in the French Quarter including a horse-drawn carriage rides, tips on the best jazz clubs or transportation to your event. All this and more sums up the hospitality that the Ambassadors Royl Hotel famous for.
