Meeting and Events

For all gala dinners, prestigious awards nights, your own Royal Wedding, Ladies Lunch or any special corporate or family occasion, the Ambassadors Royal Hotel has a range of banqueting facilities that can cater for your own special event or occasion in our unique way. Our main ballroom, the Carlisle Suite has been recently refurbished & can seat up to 450 people for gala banqueting events, while our more intimate deluxe room can cater for private dinners for up to 140 people. Laurels Bar also provides a less formal setting for dinners and drinks receptions. All of our event rooms are equipped with their own private bars and the latest Audio Visual facilities to assist you in creating your own special atmosphere and occasion. Our banqueting co-ordinators will also assist you with every detail in the lead up to your event and our senior management oversee the delivery of all banqueting occasions held in the hotel. Our main ballroom, the Carlisle suite enjoys unrivalled views across Nairobi and this view is spectacular both during the day and at night .

We specialise in the following banqueting events:

Gala Dinners
Awards Lunches and Dinners
Corporate Dinners and hospitality
Formal & Informal Drinks Receptions
Charity Fundraising Balls
Weddings
College Graduation Ceremonies & Balls
