OUR ROOMS AND SUITES

The historic 5 Star Ambassadors Royal Hotel in Nairobi features 228 luxury bedrooms that have been updated with both contemporary and traditional furnishings & decor. Our comfort driven ‘pillow top’ beds and Egyptian Cotton Linen provides the basis for the perfect night’s sleep and our rooms are finished in soft & lavish tones of warm brown and cream while the bright autumnal colours in our executive rooms provides a contemporary yet classic feel to this room type. Many of our rooms also feature breath-taking views across Nairobi and Mombasa.

DELUX KING LEISURE KING ROOM SUITE 330

The Ambassadors Royal Hotel provides a number of choices for families who wish to stay, relax and enjoy the hotel. These include our Executive Family Rooms and a limited number of larger Family Suites. Our Executive Family rooms consist of our King sized Pillow Top Beds with sofa pull out sofa beds and extra roll away beds can be placed upon request. Our Family suites can only be booked by reserving them directly with the hotel and these large suites include a King Sized Bed, Two Single Beds and Sofa Bed for larger families who require additional space to relax in.

DELUX ONE BEDROOM

Our executive bedrooms are elegantly and simply designed for our guests who desire more spacious surroundings in which to relax and enjoy the environment of the hotel or catch up on office work in the evening while staying in the hotel. Ideal for the business guest and leisure traveller alike with soft brown tones, our executive rooms provide the environment and time for our guests to relax and enjoy the added space in each room.
